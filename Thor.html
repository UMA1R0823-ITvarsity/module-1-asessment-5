THOR

bio

Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, the son of Odin and Gaea, and the crown prince of Asgard. He wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, which grants him the ability to fly and manipulate weather among other superhuman attributes. Thor is known for his immense strength, bravery, and sense of honor. Throughout his adventures, he has fought alongside the Avengers to protect Earth and the Nine Realms from various threats.
